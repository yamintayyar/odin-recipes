Spaghetti

Description

Put tomato sauce, garlic, meat and olive oil in a pan and stir on high heat until simmering. Then boil spaghetti for 15 minutes, and move it into the pan with simmering sauce. Cook for ~10 minutes until ready.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Meat
  • Olive oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Water
