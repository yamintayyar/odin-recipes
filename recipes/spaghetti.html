Spaghetti

Description

Delicious spaghetti with tomato and meat, perfect for when you don't feel like cooking a big meal.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Meat
  • Olive oil
  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  1. Put tomato sauce, garlic, meat and olive oil in a pan and stir on high heat until simmering.
  2. Boil spaghetti for 15 minutes
  3. Move spaghetti into the pan with simmering sauce. Cook for ~10 minutes until ready.
